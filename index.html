About The Owner

Cybill E. Valentine, CMP

Cybill E. Valentine, CMP is a highly skilled meetings professional with over eighteen years of experience in association management. Collaborating with many stakeholders, including executives, volunteers, board members and staff, Cybill has overseen the planning and implementation of educational events ranging from 20-30,000 attendees.

Her experience in managing a wide variety of organizations with a positive attitude, passion for client service and willingness to do whatever it takes has lead her to a successful career is meeting and conference management for more than a decade.
